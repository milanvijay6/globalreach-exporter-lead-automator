## üîç What This Means

**"Business portfolio required"** means you need:
1. A **Meta Business Portfolio** (or Business Account)
2. A **WhatsApp Business Account** connected to it
3. Your app connected to the Business Portfolio

---

## ‚úÖ Step-by-Step Setup

### **Step 1: Create/Verify Meta Business Account**

1. **Go to Meta Business Manager:**
   - Visit: https://business.facebook.com
   - Sign in with your Facebook account

2. **Check if you have a Business Account:**
   - If you see a dashboard, you have one ‚úÖ
   - If not, create one:
     - Click **"Create Account"** or **"Get Started"**
     - Follow the setup wizard
     - Enter your business details

---

### **Step 2: Set Up WhatsApp Business Account**

You need a WhatsApp Business Account in your Business Portfolio:

#### **Option A: Use Existing WhatsApp Business Number**

If you already have a WhatsApp Business number:

1. **Go to Meta Business Manager:**
   - Visit: https://business.facebook.com
   - Navigate to **"WhatsApp Accounts"** (in left sidebar)

2. **Add Your WhatsApp Number:**
   - Click **"Add"** or **"Connect"**
   - Enter your WhatsApp Business phone number
   - Verify the number (you'll receive a code)
   - Complete the setup

#### **Option B: Get a New WhatsApp Business Number**

If you don't have a WhatsApp Business number:

1. **Go to Meta Business Manager:**
   - Visit: https://business.facebook.com
   - Navigate to **"WhatsApp Accounts"**

2. **Get Started:**
   - Click **"Get Started"** or **"Add WhatsApp Account"**
   - Choose:
     - **Free tier** - For testing (limited messages)
     - **Paid tier** - For production (pay per message)

3. **Follow the Setup:**
   - Enter your business details
   - Verify your phone number
   - Complete the registration

---

### **Step 3: Connect Your App to Business Portfolio**

1. **Go to Meta for Developers:**
   - Visit: https://developers.facebook.com/apps
   - Select your app

2. **Add WhatsApp Product:**
   - If you see the WhatsApp setup page, click **"Set Up"** or **"Get Started"**
   - If not, look for **"Add Product"** ‚Üí **"WhatsApp"**

3. **Connect to Business Portfolio:**
   - During setup, you'll be asked to select your Business Portfolio
   - Select the Business Account you created/verified
   - Connect your WhatsApp Business Account

---

### **Step 4: Complete WhatsApp Setup**

After connecting to Business Portfolio:

1. **Follow the Setup Wizard:**
   - It will guide you through the setup
   - You'll need to:
     - Select your WhatsApp Business Account
     - Configure webhooks
     - Get your credentials

2. **Get Your Credentials:**
   - **Access Token** - For API calls
   - **Phone Number ID** - Your WhatsApp Business number ID
   - **Business Account ID** - Your Business Account ID

## üîß Troubleshooting

### **Issue: "Business portfolio required" Error**

**Cause:** Your app isn't connected to a Business Portfolio

**Fix:**
1. Make sure you have a Meta Business Account
2. Make sure you have a WhatsApp Business Account
3. Connect your app to the Business Portfolio during setup

### **Issue: Can't Find Business Portfolio**

**Fix:**
1. Go to: https://business.facebook.com
2. Create a Business Account if you don't have one
3. Make sure you're using the same Facebook account

### **Issue: No WhatsApp Business Account**

**Fix:**
1. Go to Meta Business Manager
2. Navigate to WhatsApp Accounts
3. Add your WhatsApp Business number
4. Or get a new WhatsApp Business number
